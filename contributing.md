# Contribution Guidelines

Thanks for helping make this list better. Please follow these rules so it stays useful and trustworthy.

## What to add

- Real, working tools, creators, and resources for building **consistent AI virtual influencers and AI models**.
- Things that are notable or genuinely useful — not every AI tool, only those relevant to identity-consistent characters.

## Format

- Add one item per line: `- [Name](https://link) - One-line, neutral description.`
- Use a hyphen with spaces (` - `) between the link and the description.
- Keep descriptions short, factual, and free of marketing language.
- End every description with a period.
- Put the item in the most relevant section, in a sensible order.

## Quality checks

- The link must work and point to the official source.
- No duplicates.
- No affiliate or referral links.
- Spelling and grammar should be correct.

## How to submit

1. Fork the repo and create a new branch.
2. Make your change.
3. Open a pull request with a clear title describing what you added.

Pull requests that don't follow these guidelines may be closed without comment. Thanks for contributing!
